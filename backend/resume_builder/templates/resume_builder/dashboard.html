Tailor for Each Job

Customize your resume for each position by highlighting relevant skills and experiences that match the job description.

Use Action Verbs

Keywords Matter

Include industry-specific keywords to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) that screen resumes.

Eliminate typos and grammatical errors by carefully reviewing your resume or having someone else look it over.
